# GlowRoot Skincare — Safety Policy

## General Safety Guidelines

- All GlowRoot products are dermatologically tested.
- Perform a **patch test** before first use of any new product: apply a small amount to the inside of your wrist and wait 24 hours. If irritation occurs, discontinue use.
- Avoid contact with eyes. In case of contact, rinse thoroughly with water.
- Keep products out of reach of children.
- Store products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

## Allergen Information

- Our products are free from parabens, sulfates (SLS/SLES), and artificial fragrances.
- Some products contain natural botanical extracts. Please check the ingredient list if you have known plant-based allergies.
- Full ingredient lists are available on each product page on our website.

## Medical Disclaimer

- GlowRoot Skincare products are cosmetic products and are **not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition**.
- If you have a skin condition (e.g., eczema, psoriasis, rosacea), consult a dermatologist before using our products.
- Our customer support team cannot provide medical advice. Any skin health concerns should be discussed with a qualified healthcare professional.
- Claims about product benefits refer to cosmetic improvements only and are not medical claims.

## Product Recalls

- In the unlikely event of a product recall, affected customers will be notified directly via WhatsApp and email with instructions for returns and replacements.

## Reporting Issues

- If you experience any adverse reaction, please contact us immediately at safety@glowroot.in or through WhatsApp.
