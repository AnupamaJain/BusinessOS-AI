# GlowRoot Skincare — Shipping Policy

## Domestic Shipping (India)

- **Standard Delivery**: 5–7 business days. Free for orders above ₹999.
- **Express Delivery**: 2–3 business days. ₹149 flat rate.
- **Same-Day Delivery**: Available in select metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune). ₹249 flat rate. Orders placed before 12:00 PM IST.

## Order Processing

- Orders are processed within 24 hours of placement (excluding Sundays and public holidays).
- Tracking details are shared via WhatsApp and email once the order is dispatched.

## International Shipping

- International shipping is not currently available. We plan to launch international delivery in the next quarter.

## Shipping Partners

- We use trusted logistics partners including BlueDart, DTDC, and Delhivery.

## Delivery Issues

- If your order has not arrived within the estimated window, please contact us through WhatsApp or email at support@glowroot.in.
- We are unable to redirect orders once dispatched. Please ensure the delivery address is correct before placing an order.
