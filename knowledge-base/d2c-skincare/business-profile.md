# GlowRoot Skincare — Business Profile

## About GlowRoot

GlowRoot Skincare is an Indian direct-to-consumer skincare brand focused on effective, clean, and affordable skincare for everyday use. Founded in 2023, we believe everyone deserves access to scientifically-backed skincare without harmful ingredients.

## Our Values

- **Clean formulations**: No parabens, sulfates, or artificial fragrances.
- **Science-backed**: Every product is developed with dermatologist input and clinically tested ingredients.
- **Affordable luxury**: Premium quality at accessible price points.
- **Sustainability**: Recyclable packaging and cruelty-free formulations.
