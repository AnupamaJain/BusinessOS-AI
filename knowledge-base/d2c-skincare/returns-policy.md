# GlowRoot Skincare — Returns & Refund Policy

## Return Window

- Products may be returned within **15 days** of delivery.
- The product must be unused, unopened, and in its original packaging.

## Non-Returnable Items

- Products that have been opened or used.
- Products purchased during clearance or promotional sales (unless defective).
- Gift cards.

## How to Initiate a Return

1. Contact us via WhatsApp or email at support@glowroot.in with your order number.
2. Our team will review your request and provide a return shipping label if approved.
3. Ship the product back using the provided label.

## Refund Process

- Refunds are processed within **7–10 business days** after we receive the returned product.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defective product or our error.

## Damaged or Defective Products

- If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within **48 hours** of delivery with photos.
- We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost.

## Exchanges

- Exchanges are available for size or variant changes, subject to stock availability.
- Contact our support team to arrange an exchange.

## Important Note

- GlowRoot Skincare customer support can assist with return and refund enquiries, but all refund approvals and processing are handled by our support team, not by automated systems. If you require a refund, you will be connected to a human agent.
